return policy

We stand behind our quality — returns accepted only for damaged/defective items.

effective: 7 day window

7-day window

We accept return requests within 7 calendar days from delivery. This policy is strict — please inspect your order immediately upon arrival.

⚠️ Important: Returns are accepted ONLY for damaged or defective products. No change of mind, no "didn't like it," and no returns for minor scratches on the outer box.

eligibility criteria

eligible

  • Product physically damaged
  • Defective / non-functional
  • Wrong item shipped
  • Within 7 days of delivery
  • Original sealed condition

not eligible

  • Change of mind
  • Minor box scratches
  • After 7 days
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Missing unboxing video

mandatory evidence

Continuous unboxing video — from sealed package to product reveal, no cuts.

Clear photos of the damage on the product itself (not just the box).

Order number visible in video/photos.

Claims without video evidence cannot be processed. This protects both you and us from fraud.

return process

1
Document the damage

Record unboxing video + take photos immediately after delivery.

2
Email us

Send evidence + order number to returns@myessantia.com

3
Get approval

We review within 24-48 hours and respond with next steps.

4
Ship back (if approved)

We provide a prepaid return label. Ship item in original packaging.

refunds & replacements

  • Replacement: We ship a new item immediately upon receiving the damaged return (if in stock).
  • Refund: Full refund to original payment method within 5-7 business days after we receive the return.
  • Shipping: Return shipping is covered by us for approved damage claims.

important notes

  • Outer box damage without product damage is not eligible for return.
  • Items must be returned in original condition with all tags and packaging.
  • If you send a non-returnable item, return shipping cost will be deducted from refund.
  • We reserve the right to reject returns that don't meet these strict criteria.
